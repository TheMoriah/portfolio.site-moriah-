Who is Moriah?

My Story

The first time I used Microsoft Word, back in elementary school, I spent 20 minutes picking out the font and deciding how I wanted my document setup. In highschool I learned that you can get paid for keeping that same level of detail as a graphic designer. I worked as a graphic designer for 6 years and designed lots of layouts and logos. Product design is the newest addition to my design skill set, which I want to continue growing. Understanding the user and their needs to craft a design specifically for those needs has been my favorite part about product design. With these skills, I make a full brand identity consisting of printed materials, digital materials, applications and websites consistent with a style guide. Working on a design brings me back to when I was a kid crafting the best document.
