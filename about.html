Who is Moriah?

Hello! I’m a digital designer that has experience in graphic design and product design, looking to bring an identity to life with design. My goal when designing is to create products aligned with a style guide used on printed materials, digital materials, applications and websites. In my designs, I take into consideration user research and usability testing to affirm the design decisions are optimized for the user and free from usability issues. With each project, I enjoy improving my own abilities of creative problem solving to craft a user-centered experience. I’m looking forward to opportunities to work and grow in my abilities to offer user-centered designs.
